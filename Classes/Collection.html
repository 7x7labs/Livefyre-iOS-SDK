Overview

Collection represents a single (possibly anonymous) user’s view of a Livefyre collection of comments for an article.

When initially created, collections have the metadata fields populated, but none of the contents such as authors and posts. To retrieve the contents, fetchBootstrap: can be called to retrieve the newest posts in the collection, or fetchPage:gotPage: or fetchRange:gotRange: can be called to retrieve a specific range of posts.

After retrieving the content, the top-level comments to the article will be found in the posts property, with replies to those comments in those Posts' children property.

fetchRange:gotRange:

Fetch all posts within the given date range

- (DateRange *)fetchRange:(DateRange *)range gotRange:(RequestComplete)callback

Parameters

range

Range to retrieve posts for

callback

Callback called with the array of new posts found in that date range. May be called multiple times if the requested time range requires multiple requests to retrieve, or zero times if there are no posts within the given range which have not been retrieved already.

Return Value

The date range which was actually requested from the server.

Unlike fetchPage:gotPage:, calling this with parameters which will not result in any data being fetched is not an error.

Discussion
